Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs)

CLO 4 Utilize the knowledge to solve problems and build own project

About This Course

An OBE-structured 11-week course covering the full Big Data stack — from the distributed file system foundations (HDFS, GFS) through batch and stream processing (MapReduce, Spark, Kafka), SQL-on-Hadoop (Hive, Presto), visualization, and real-world application domains (social media analytics, time-series forecasting, healthcare informatics).

Benchmarked against Stanford CS246, Harvard CS265, MIT 6.830, and CMU 15-721. Every week has two Beamer lecture decks: Part 1 (core content & assessment) and Part 2 (seminal paper deep-dive & industry case study).
